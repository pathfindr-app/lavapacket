Why Aluminum for Hawaii?
Hawaii's coastal environment is tough on roofing materials. Salt air corrodes steel and degrades asphalt. Aluminum standing seam is naturally corrosion-resistant — it forms a protective oxide layer that actually strengthens over time. Combined with a Kynar paint finish, your roof will look stunning for 30+ years.
